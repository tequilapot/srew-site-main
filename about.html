Premium Manufacturing & Engineering Excellence

S R ENGG WORKS (SREW) is a premier manufacturer specializing in the end-to-end design, manufacturing, and turnkey installation of high-capacity crushing, screening, and material handling plants. We serve top-tier operators across the mining, aggregate, and infrastructure sectors with a relentless commitment to build quality and operational uptime.

Our integrated ecosystem—featuring CNC machining, heavy-duty fabrication, and PLC automation—ensures that every plant meets rigorous global standards for capacity, efficiency, and real-world performance.

30+ Years Experience

Leadership

Mr. Ranga Reddy J.

Founder & Managing Director

With over 30 years of field-tested expertise, Ranga Reddy leads SREW with a focus on reliability and execution quality. His extensive career includes serving as Operations Head for major projects in Kenya and key roles at Srisailam and Singareni Collieries.

Having managed industrial operations across India, Africa, and beyond, he bridges local conditions with international standards to deliver practical, robust engineering solutions. A recipient of the 'Best Engineer Award' by Balaji Industries, his hands-on leadership at SREW ensures every project is built for durability and long-term performance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about S R ENGG WORKS

When was SREW established?

S R ENGG WORKS (SREW) was established in 2018, bringing over 30 years of combined industry experience to the metals and mining sector.

What are your facilities like?

Our facility in Hyderabad includes CNC machining centers, fabrication bays, hydraulic press shops, and PLC panel manufacturing units.

How many projects delivered?

We have successfully delivered over 120 turnkey projects for 45+ clients across India and internationally in the mining and recycling sectors.

Who is the SREW founder?

Mr. Ranga Reddy J., Founder & Managing Director, brings over 30 years of hands-on expertise from major global industrial projects.

What is your design philosophy?

We focus on reliability, efficiency, and execution quality, ensuring that every plant is built for maximum durability and uptime.

Are you ISO certified?

Yes, S R ENGG WORKS is an ISO 9001:2015 certified company, adhering to the highest international quality management standards.

What sectors do you serve?

We primarily serve the Mining, Construction, Infrastructure, and C&D Waste Recycling industries with specialized engineering.

Do you offer custom builds?

Absolutely. Every plant we design—from 30 TPH to 800 TPH—is custom-built to meet the specific material and operational needs of the client.
