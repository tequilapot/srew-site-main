Who We Are

S R ENGG WORKS (SREW) is a precision engineering and industrial fabrication company, specializing in the design, manufacture, installation, and servicing of crushing, screening, sand processing, and material handling plants.

Headquartered in India, SREW serves clients across mining, construction, infrastructure, and recycling sectors with a reputation for reliability, innovation, and customization.

Our state-of-the-art facilities include CNC machines, high-precision fabrication bays, hydraulic press shops, and PLC panel manufacturing units. All systems are quality certified and optimized for long-term field performance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about S R ENGG WORKS

When was S R ENGG WORKS established?

S R ENGG WORKS (SREW) was established in 2018. Our founding team brings over 30 years of combined industry experience in precision engineering, industrial fabrication, and heavy equipment manufacturing for the mining, construction, and recycling sectors.

What are SREW's manufacturing facilities?

SREW operates a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Hyderabad, India, equipped with CNC machining centers for precision component manufacturing, high-precision fabrication bays for large-scale industrial structures, hydraulic press shops for metal forming, and PLC panel manufacturing units for plant automation.

How many projects has SREW delivered?

SREW has successfully delivered over 120 turnkey projects for 45+ clients across India. Our projects span mining, construction, infrastructure, sand processing, and C&D waste recycling applications, with plant capacities ranging from 30 TPH to 800 TPH.
