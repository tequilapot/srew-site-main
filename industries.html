Mining Industry

S R ENGG WORKS delivers heavy-duty, reliable crushing and screening solutions designed for the demanding conditions of the mining industry. Our plants are engineered for high throughput, durability, and continuous operation.

Key Solutions for Mining:

  • High-capacity Jaw Crushers for primary breaking.
  • Robust Cone Crushers for secondary and tertiary crushing.
  • Heavy-duty Vibrating Screens for precise sizing.
  • Durable Grizzly Feeders for pre-screening and feeding.

Sand Processing & Manufacturing

We specialize in equipment for manufacturing high-quality sand, including M-Sand for construction and specialized dried sand for foundries, glass manufacturing, and brick making.

Key Solutions for Sand Processing:

  • VSI/HSI Crushers for producing cubical M-Sand.
  • Efficient Sand Dryers (LPG/Gas/Diesel fired) for moisture control.
  • Vibrating Screens for precise sand classification.
  • Sand Washing Plants (Spiral Washers).
  • Silos for storing processed sand.

Industry & Customer FAQs

Questions from quarry operators, contractors, M-Sand producers & EPC partners

What industries do you serve?

S R ENGG WORKS serves mining, highway construction, M-Sand producers, C&D waste recycling, and EPC firms with turnkey solutions.

Highway project plants?

For NHAI highway projects, we recommend 200–800 TPH plants with Jaw and Cone crushers for meeting IRC specifications.

M-Sand plant setup?

We provide complete turnkey M-Sand plants including VSI crushers, screens, sand dryers, and silos for moisture-controlled output.

Hard rock quarry plants?

For granite and basalt quarries, we supply multi-stage circuits with heavy-duty jaw and cone crushers for 24/7 operation.

EPC partnership model?

We serve as the equipment manufacturing arm for EPC firms, providing white-label or co-branded crushing and screening plants.

Smart City projects?

We supply C&D waste recycling plants compliant with Swachh Bharat and Smart Cities Mission green norms and CPCB guidelines.

C&D waste recycling?

Our integrated C&D recycling plants handle 30-100 TPH, including sorting, crushing, and separating materials for reuse.

Mining equipment supply?

We supply high-capacity Jaw crushers, Grizzly feeders, and Conveyor systems engineered for heavy mining and quarrying applications.

Plant lead times?

Standard lead time for a complete turnkey plant is 8–12 weeks, including design, fabrication, testing, and on-site commissioning.
