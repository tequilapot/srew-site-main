Spare Parts FAQs

Common questions about our spare parts and wear components

What spares do you supply?

SREW supplies jaw plates, cone liners, blow bars, screen meshes, rollers, and bearings for all major crusher brands.

Jaw plates and liners?

Yes, we manufacture high-manganese steel jaw plates, mantles, and concaves for maximum durability and wear life.

Compatibility with others?

We supply precision-engineered parts compatible with all major Indian and international crusher and screen manufacturers.

Conveyor components?

We maintain an inventory of heavy-duty conveyor belts, idlers, rollers, and pulleys for high-capacity material handling systems.

Screen mesh options?

Our screen meshes are available in spring steel, stainless steel, and polyurethane for precise grading in various applications.

Electrical spare parts?

We supply PLC modules, VFDs, push buttons, and sensors for crushing plant control panels and automation systems.

Lead time for parts?

Standard consumable parts are in stock for immediate dispatch, while custom wear parts typically take 2-4 weeks.

Quality of wear parts?

All SREW wear parts are manufactured using high-grade alloys and precision casting to ensure long service life in tough conditions.
