Spare Parts FAQs

Common questions about our spare parts and wear components

What spare parts does SREW supply?

S R ENGG WORKS supplies a comprehensive range of spare parts for crushing and screening equipment including jaw plates, toggle plates, cheek plates for jaw crushers; mantles, concaves, and bowl liners for cone crushers; blow bars, anvils, and rotor tips for impact crushers; screen meshes, deck panels, and rubber products for vibrating screens; and conveyor belts, rollers, and bearings for material handling systems.

Does SREW provide jaw plates and cone liners?

Yes, SREW manufactures and supplies high-quality jaw plates (fixed and movable) in manganese steel, as well as cone liners including mantles, concaves, and bowl liners for all major crusher brands and models. All wear parts are manufactured using high manganese steel and precision casting for maximum durability.

Can SREW supply parts for other manufacturer's equipment?

Yes, SREW supplies compatible spare parts for all major crusher and screen brands operating in India. We manufacture OEM-equivalent parts engineered to match or exceed original specifications, ensuring proper fit and extended wear life for your existing equipment.
