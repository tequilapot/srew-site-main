Product FAQs

Common questions about our crushing, screening & processing equipment

What types of crushers does SREW manufacture?

S R ENGG WORKS manufactures four types of crushers: Jaw Crushers (30-800 TPH) for primary rock breaking, Cone Crushers (50-500 TPH) for secondary aggregate shaping, VSI/HSI Crushers (50-300 TPH) for M-Sand and cubical aggregate production, and Roller Crushers (50-100 TPH) for fine crushing of soft materials like coal, clay, and limestone.

What is the capacity range of SREW jaw crushers?

SREW jaw crushers are available from 30 TPH to 800 TPH capacity. They feature heavy-duty construction, high reduction ratio, adjustable Closed Side Setting (CSS), and optional hydraulic toggle systems. Applications include mining, road construction, cement plants, and rock crushing.

Does SREW manufacture sand dryers?

Yes, SREW manufactures rotary sand dryers with 20-50 TPH capacity. They are available in LPG, Natural Gas, and Diesel-fired options with automatic temperature control, cyclone dust separation, and adjustable heat zones that maintain moisture levels under 2%. Used in foundries, ready-mix plants, and glass manufacturing.

What is a VSI crusher used for?

A VSI (Vertical Shaft Impact) crusher is used for producing cubical manufactured sand (M-Sand) and high-quality aggregates. SREW VSI crushers use rock-on-rock or rock-on-anvil crushing principles at high RPM for superior particle shape. They are used in M-Sand manufacturing, plaster sand production, asphalt mixes, and concrete production.
